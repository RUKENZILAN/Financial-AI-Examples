Executive Signal Panel
✔ Strength
Revenue Momentum
Revenue surged 28.2% to TRY 955.5bn, with EBITDA reaching a record TRY 184.8bn (+21%). Interest coverage strengthened from 7.2x to 8.6x, signalling robust debt-service capacity despite elevated leverage.
⚠ Monitor
Margin Compression
Gross margin contracted 290 bps to 16.3%; net margin fell from 15.2% to 12.4%. COGS growth (+32.8%) outpaced revenue, driven by fuel, maintenance and FX-linked cost inflation. EBITDA margin held near 19%.
⚑ Watch
Rising Leverage
Net Debt/EBITDA climbed sharply from 2.58x to 3.67x. CapEx doubled to TRY 80.1bn (+82%), pushing investing cash flow deep negative at −TRY 144.7bn. Lease obligations (ST+LT) reached TRY 639bn.
ℹ Note
Equity & Dividends
Total equity grew 34% to TRY 911.3bn, partly driven by FX translation reserves. First-ever dividend payment of TRY 8.95bn signals capital confidence. Equity ratio modestly declined to 45.6% as assets expanded faster.
